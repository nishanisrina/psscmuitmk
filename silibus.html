PSSCM Syllabus



Basic level (Peringkat asas)

Students are introduced to the 4 Silat Cekak methods, which names as A, B, C and D. Method A is inspired by doa (pray), method B is inspired by qiam, method C is inspired by bowing (rukuk), and method D is inspired by takbiratulram. Students are then introduced to the 21 basic components of the A, B, C and D methods; trained to move both hands and feet.

21 names of the movements in Seni Silat Celak :

  1. Buah Pertama
  2. Buah Keputusan Gayung Fatani
  3. Buah Keputusan Sendeng Atas
  4. Buah Kilas Belakang
  5. Buah Kilas Hadapan
  6. Buah Kidong Kiri
  7. Buah Kuntau Jatuh
  8. Buah Kuntau Kiri & Kuntau Kiri Gantung
  9. Buah Kuntau Kanan Gantung
  10. Buah Hentak (Keputusan Silat Harimau)
  11. Buah Ali Patah Atas
  12. Buah Ali Patah Sudah
  13. Buah Selendang
  14. Buah Gari & Gari Gunting
  15. Buah Paras
  16. Buah Keputusan Pasung Kemanga
  17. Buah Keputusan Terlak
  18. Buah Keputusan Lintau
  19. Buah Hempok
  20. Buah Luncur
  21. Buah Siku Potong



Falling Level (Peringkat Jatuh)

The fraction of each movements of the basic lessons are introduced in the second stage. With these basic movements, it is possible to defend against all kinds of attacks. The basic movements and every fractions are the movements of all knowledge results across the Malay martial arts in the Malay world as 'Keputusan Sendeng', 'Keputusan Gayung Fatani', 'Keputusan Silat Harimau' and others as mentioned at the beginning.

Cut Level (Peringkat Potong)

This stage is the secret stage. Students are exposed to learning how to cut or block all 21 of the above. Unless the martial arts practitioner attacks and the enemy uses one of these, the movement is not capable of locking or capturing the martial arts; even the Seni Silat Cekak can attack back with any of these movements. This is where the Seni Silat Cekak philosophy goes:

"PAKAI TAK PAKAI, TAK PAKAI PAKAI " (use but not using, not using but use)

Practitioner SKill Level

At this stage, students will be taught how to repel attacks with their bare hands when they are attacked either by bare hands of other martial arts practitioners or with weapons. Upon completion of this course, students will be required to sit for the Lower Certificate of Examination and upon graduation, they will receive the Silat Cekak Certificate at the annual Silat Cekak Convocation Ceremony

(Source : https://www.silatcekak.org.my/matapelajaran/ )
