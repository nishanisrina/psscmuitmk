Definition of Cekak

  • Old Word


    • The Cekak is an old word that was born since the days of the Malay community. The word 'Cekak' is used on a daily basis, it reflects the close relationship between our community and the users of 'Cekak' as long as they are far from understanding. Due to the Cekak methods are related to Malay grammar, to find out why Silat Cekak is called 'Silat Cekai', we must look at in terms of terminology and the use of 'Cekak' itself.

  • Makan Cekak

    • In the North especially in Kedah, the word Cekak has become a language synonym - 'makan cekak'. The language carries the meaning of responsibility. Suppose there's a job to do, then someone says:
    "Ah, if that's only (done by) me then 'makan cekak'. (“Ah, itu aku seorang saja pun ‘makan cekak’.”)"

    When brought back to 'Silat Cekak' then the meaning of 'Silat Cekak' is the silat responsible for keeping its practitioners from being persecuted by their enemies.

  • Bercakak

    • In Minang language, the word Cekak is also present, but it is not referred to as 'Ce-kak' but is referred to as the 'Cakak'. Speaking in Minang means fighting. For example, "Ali and Ahmad are cakak at the road" (“Si Ali dan si Ahmad sedang bercakak di simpang tiga”. ). This means that Ali and Ahmad are fighting. So we come back to the meaning of the Silat Silat - it carries the meaning of 'Silat Gaduh'. This does not mean that Silat Cekak likes to fight regularly but it is Silat that can be fought to defend yourself. Obviously Silat Cekak is actually Silat that is really about martial arts not for performances at banquets or parties. That's why Silat Cekak has no dance moves, drums and step by step. It only advances when fighting with enemies.

  • Secekak

    • Then let's go to the Cekak word. It carries the meaning of grabbing. This grabbing, in turn, has many meanings. First, Silat Cekak learns something that is not something that grabbing (small piece) and not grabbing the whole (amount). Here it is intended that the Silat Cekak subject is only the subject of silat (a small grab) rather than the numerous branches (huge amount of a grab). Because Silat Cekak is a study of movement, it is very fast, organized and can be taught in a very short time. Second, a grab shows solidity. So Silat Cekak is a very strict and standalone Silat. Where the rules give the practitioners confidence that they have found secrets and something useful in the field of martial arts.

Conclusion

From the foregoing points we can draw a conclusion, why Silat Cekak is called Silat Cekak. Silat is named according to the practice of Silat Martial Arts because this is the only type of martial arts that are genuine inheritance rights for Malays who have a stake in keeping themselves accountable practitioners. For clarification, the above description simply emphasizes Silat Cekak with the use of the word 'Cekak'. In fact, in terms of the subjects and methods taught, it is appropriate and precise why Silat Cekak is called Silat Cekak. This is of course known to the martial arts students who have completed their studies.
