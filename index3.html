Protocol Skillshare Day

Protocol day!, May 24, 2020



Protocol Skillshare Day Program is a workshop program or program on the description of tasks related to the protocol. The purpose of this program is to serve as a medium through exposure to UiTM Kedah Branch students to know more about the scope of management the right thing to make a program successful. In addition, the main focus of the program is to discuss and expose more in depth about the concept of protocol as it is still available some students do not understand the scope of the tasks related to the protocol in the material program. The program was attended by students of UiTM Kedah Branch as well as PSSCM members of UiTM Kedah Branch. This program are held in Dewan Sarjana 2, Uitm Cawangan Kedah and has been invited Prof. Madya Dr. Asmadi bin Mohammed Ghazali as a speaker.
