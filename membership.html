PSSCM Membership and Fees

Seni Silat Cekak Malaysia is open to all Malaysian men and women 16 years old (preferably 18 years old) to those who are Muslim and are physically fit and of sound mind.

The entire Seni Silat Cekak Malaysia lesson system is taught to the students in a package. Each student will need to complete the package first and then it will be assumed that they have studied Seni Silat Cekak Malaysia until they are responsible for their defense.

The Silat Cekak Malaysia subject system, which is divided into several levels, takes about 7 months to complete. After completing the Seni Silat Cekak Malaysia course, students are encouraged to continue their skills in Silat Cekak by attending skills classes which typically take approximately 3 months with 1 training class per week (subject to current change).

For more information related to the fee, you can go to the nearby PSSCM training class in your area.
