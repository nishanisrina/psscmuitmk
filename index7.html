7th Leadership Course PSSCM IPT National Level 2018

Leadership!, May 21, 2020



PSSCM UiTM Kedah Branch was honoured in joining invitation for a program that held by PSSCM USIM in order to establishing a good relationship between UiTM Kedah and USIM also strengthen the relationship between members of the association. This program give many benefits of which help to enhance the thinking of each member in organizing a program or event because a lot of exposure and knowledge is shared through talk sessions during the program.
