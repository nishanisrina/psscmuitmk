Charity With Love

Charity time!, May 25, 2020



On November 16, 2019, the Kedah Branch PSSCM held a Charity With Love. The Charity With Love took place for a day on November 16, 2019 in Kampung Singkir Genting Merbok, Kedah. Members of the PSSCM UiTM Kedah Branch have been working hard to make the program more friendly with the local villagers as well as doing charity work with a few donations. The program was launched by Y. Bhg. Associate Professor Dr. Shaiful Annuar Khalid. Here are the details of the program:

Tentative

16 NOVEMBER 2019 (SATURDAY)

7.30 a.m : Student attendance registration and departure
8.00 a.m : Arrival of students and invited guests
8.30 a.m : Opening ceremony
- Reciting of Do’a
- Selawat Badriah
- Sing “UiTM” song
- Speech by VIPs.
9.00 a.m : Cooperation begins
12.30 p.m : End-to-end concerts & friendly sessions
1.30 p.m : Zohor prayers and talks
2.15 p.m : Donation
2.30 p.m : Photography session
2.45 p.m : Disperse
