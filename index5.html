Kejohanan Waja Diri (KWD) Persatuan Seni Silat Cekak Malaysia (PSSCM) IPT & Jemputan Kali Ke-9

KWD!, May 23, 2020



On August 24, 2019, the PSSCM UiTm Kedah Branch held the Kejohanan Waja Diri (KWD) Persatuan Seni Silat Cekak Malaysia (PSSCM) IPT and 9th Invitation Tournament. The Self-Care Championship took place for one day on August 24, 2019 at the Chancellor's Hall, University of Selangor (Unisel). The program is organized by the Malaysian Association of Fine Arts (PSSCM) University of Selangor (Unisel) Bestari Jaya. Here are the details of the program:
