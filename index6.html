9th Karnival Sukan Tradisional (KASUTRA) 2019 IPT Level

Kasutra!, May 22, 2020



It is a traiditional sports program which every PSSCM IPT Branch would gather and participate the traditional sport games together. There were a lot of interesting activities and sports such as 'Batu Seremban', 'Galah Panjang', 'Congkak' and others. It was only held for one day at USM Main Branch.

Tentative

5 OCT 2019 (SATURDAY)

8.00 a.m : Student registration
8.30 a.m : Respected Guest Arrival
8.45 a.m. : Opening speech by emcee
8.50 a.m. : Reciting of Do’a
8.55 a.m. : Sing 'Negaraku' and 'Menara Ilmu'
9.05 a.m. : Speech by KASUTRA Leader Program, Mr. Muhammad Zarif Firdaus Bin Zakaria
9.10 a.m. : Speech by First Advisor PSSCMUSM, Prof. Madya. Dr. Abdul Hafiz Bin Abdul Majid
9.15 a.m. : Opening speech by vice Chancellor USM, YBhg. Datuk Prof Dr. Asma Ismail
9.25 a.m. : The opening gimmick begins
9.30 a.m. : Demostration performance by PSSCMUSM
9.40 a.m. : Delivery of souvenirs to VIP
10.00 a.m. : Light dish (for VIP)
1.00 p.m. : Break & Pray for Zohor
2.15 p.m. : Second game session starts
4.45 p.m. : Game ends
5.05 p.m. : Speech by YDP PSSCMUSM, Hishamuddin Bin Hashim
5.10 p.m. : Official Clossing Speech by Culture Planner Leader, Dr. Fadzilah Binti Amzah
5.20 p.m. : Prize and souvenier giving ceremony
5.50 p.m : Singing "Warisan Mutlak"
6.00 p.m : Disperse
